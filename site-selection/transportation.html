Transportation

For distribution and logistics companies, the rule of thumb is “55 in 5” – which means their trucks need to be going 55 mph in 5 minutes or less. That is no problem at Coyote Business Park, which straddles Interstate 84 at Exit 216. Other major north/south freeways and highways can be accessed within just a few miles of the park, as well Eastern Oregon Regional Airport – with daily passenger flights to/from Portland and capacity for cargo flights.
