Kayak Public Transportation

It is owned and operated by the Confederated Tribes of the Umatilla Indian Reservation, east of Pendleton, Oregon. It serves fifteen cities surrounding Pendleton Oregon. The buses stop in each city three to four times each work day. The cities include: Adams, Athena, College Place, Hermiston, Irrigon, La Grande, Meacham, Milton-Freewater, Mission, Pendleton, Pilot Rock, Stanfield, Umatilla, Walla Walla, and Weston. All rides are FREE!

For more information about Kayak transportation call (541) 429-7519 or go to the Kayak Public Transit's webpage.
