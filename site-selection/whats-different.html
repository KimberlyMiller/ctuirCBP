What’s different about locating at Coyote Business Park?

Coyote Business Park is located in Pendleton, Oregon and is owned and operated by the Confederated Tribes of the Umatilla Indian Reservation. As a general rule, conducting business on the reservation isn’t significantly different from running a business anywhere else. Businesses are subject to federal employment laws, pay state and federal taxes (check out our tax incentives!), and have access to state courts to resolve civil disputes. Read on to learn what differentiates Coyote Business Park from other properties.

Dedicated to Economic Development

Economic Impact

With an annual operating budget in excess of $220 million and nearly 1,600 employees, the Confederated Tribes of the Umatilla Indian Reservation have established themselves as a regional leader in economic development. With a mix of on- and off-reservation projects and a mix of tribal and non-Indian employees, CTUIR dollars have an impact both at home and in our neighboring communities.

Experience That Counts

In addition to Coyote Business Park, the tribes own and operate successful enterprises in a broad range of industries. Wildhorse Resort & Casino may be the most notable with a Las Vegas-style casino, hotel tower, golf course, RV Park, movie theater, and a variety of dining options. The tribe’s other enterprises include Cayuse Technologies (a business-to-business technology services company), Tamastslikt Cultural Institute (a tribal museum and cultural center), Arrowhead Travel Plaza (commercial & passenger fuel, convenience store), a farming operation, a transfer station, and more.

Professional Team

The tribes are committed to economic diversification to support new industry and jobs growth for its tribal members as well as the surrounding communities. Coyote Business Park employs an on-site property maintenance contractor who can address all tenant facility needs, from changing light bulbs and air filters to landscaping to major repairs. Maintenance staff is available for emergency facility needs 24 hours per day.
