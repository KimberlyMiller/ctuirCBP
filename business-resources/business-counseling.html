Oregon Small Business Development Center

Blue Mountain Community College in Pendleton is home to one of Oregon’s Small Business Development Centers (SBDC). Oregon’s SBDC Network provides no-cost advising to anyone who owns, operates, or is starting a small business in Oregon. In addition, the SBDC offers a variety of business related workshops, seminars and online courses.

CTUIR Business Development Services

The mission of CTUIR’s Business Development Services (BDS) is to assist Native Americans in the development and expansion of small business by providing technical assistance, business training, and individual business counseling. Business classes include “Exploring Your Business Idea” and “Indianpreneurship – A Native American Journey into Business”.

Umatilla Tribes’ Community Development Financial Institution

CTUIR is the in the process of opening a community financial development institution. The mission of the Umatilla Tribes’ CDFI is to improve the financial well-being of people in the Umatilla Indian Reservation community through loans and development services, such as business planning, credit counseling, and homebuyer classes to help borrowers use credit effectively to build personal assets and wealth.
