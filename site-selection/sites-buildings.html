Coyote Business Park

Located in Pendleton, Oregon at I-84 exit 216, Coyote Business Park has lots zoned light industrial and commercial/retail. With a diverse tenant base that includes technology, medical, logistics support services, retail, office buildings, tourism and hospitality Coyote Business Park has established itself as an economic hub for the entire region. Flat sites with utilities at the curb are ready for your next project!


Wanapa Industrial Site

Wanapa Industrial site is heavy industrial zoned land adjacent to the Port of Umatilla on the Columbia River. The Wanapa Industrial Site is part of our reservation enterprise zone and therefore provides tenants the same tax advantages that tenants of Coyote Business Park enjoy.

  • 120 acre greenfield site four miles from the interstate.
  • Close to Port of Umatilla barge and rail access, a natural gas hub, and a BPA substation.
  • Air permitting is through the EPA Regional Office and the site is classified as an attainment area.
  • $3.5 million infrastructure development completed August 2018.
