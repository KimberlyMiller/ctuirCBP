Utilities

Coyote Business Park’s industrial sites have been certified under Oregon’s Certified Industrial Lands program, one of the most comprehensive shovel-ready programs in the country. Among the many requirements of the program are letters from utility providers affirming that utilities are at the site and meet the capacity requirements for the intended industrial profile.

Utilities Specifications Provider
